Introduction to ISO/SAE 21434:2021

This training aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of ISO/SAE 21434:2021 and its application in the automotive industry. It is specifically designed for IOT developers to enhance their knowledge and skills in implementing and maintaining cybersecurity measures in accordance with this standard.

INTRODUCTION

This training aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of ISO/SAE 21434:2021 and its application in the automotive industry. It is specifically designed for IOT developers to enhance their knowledge and skills in implementing and maintaining cybersecurity measures in accordance with this standard.

PARTS

An overview of ISO/SAE 21434:2021 and its clauses.

Insight into the management of cybersecurity at an organisational level.

Understanding the cybersecurity management in relation to specific projects.

Exploring the concept development phase of a product.

Understanding the product development phase and its cybersecurity considerations.

Insights into the post development phase and related cybersecurity activities.

Understanding the process of responding to cybersecurity incidents.

Learning about threat and risk analysis in the context of cybersecurity.

Practical examples and case studies of implementing cybersecurity.

Understanding the process of validating cybersecurity measures.

Insight into the ongoing activities required for maintaining cybersecurity.

CONCLUSION

This training provides a comprehensive understanding of ISO/SAE 21434:2021 and its application in the automotive industry. It equips IOT developers with the necessary knowledge and skills to implement and maintain cybersecurity measures in accordance with this standard.
