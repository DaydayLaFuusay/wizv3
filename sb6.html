You’ve Been Exploited. Now What?

This section provides guidance on how to respond effectively when a cybersecurity incident occurs in the post development phase.

  • 1Internal Cybersecurity Team: Contact the internal cybersecurity team immediately for assistance and guidance.
  • 2Incident Response Plan: Refer to the organization’s incident response plan for detailed instructions.
  • 3ISO/SAE 21434:2021 Standard: Consult the standard for best practices in responding to cybersecurity incidents.
  • 4External Cybersecurity Consultants: If necessary, engage external consultants for specialized assistance.
